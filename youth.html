Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is a vibrant space for young people to grow spiritually, emotionally, and socially. We aim to raise godly leaders grounded in Biblical truth.

About This Group

The ACK St. Faith’s Youth Ministry brings together young people between the ages 20 to 35. The group is centered on nurturing faith, leadership, talent, and service through worship, outreach, mentorship, and empowerment programs. We believe in raising a generation of courageous, Christ-centered youth ready to impact the world.

Empowering the Next Generation

We meet every Sunday and throughout the week for various activities including Bible study, mentorship, worship practice, and fun outings.

  • Youth Worship & Bible Study
  • Leadership Training
  • Mission Trips
  • Career and Life Guidance
