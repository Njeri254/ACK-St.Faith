Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is a vibrant space for young people to grow spiritually, emotionally, and socially. We aim to raise godly leaders grounded in Biblical truth.

Empowering the Next Generation

We meet every Sunday and throughout the week for various activities including Bible study, mentorship, worship practice, and fun outings.

  • Youth Worship & Bible Study
  • Leadership Training
  • Career and Life Guidance
