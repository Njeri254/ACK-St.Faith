Choir Ministry

The Choir Ministry of ACK St. Faith’s Church is at the heart of our church family, uniting all generations in joyful, Spirit-filled music. Our purpose is to glorify God through hymns, psalms, lirtugical music and songs that ushers the congregation into His presence. Whether through traditional hymns, or moments of deep reflection, our ministry seeks to lead the church into authentic encounters with God. We welcome every voice, every instrument, and every willing heart to join in lifting high the name of Jesus. Together, we worship in unity—young and old, new believers and seasoned saints—because worship is for everyone. leads the congregation in worship through music and song. We are dedicated to glorifying God through harmonies that uplift and inspire.

Praise and Worship Ministry

Praise and Worship is not just music — it is a lifestyle of honoring God with all that we are. At ACK St. Faith’s Church, our Praise and Worship ministry leads the congregation into heartfelt, Spirit-filled worship that exalts the name of Jesus.

Through songs, instruments, and sincere devotion, we prepare hearts for the Word, create space for the Holy Spirit, and glorify God with excellence, humility, and unity. The Praise & Worship Ministry exists to lead the congregation into the presence of God through heartfelt praise and Spirit-led worship. We believe that worship is not just about music—it’s a lifestyle of surrender, gratitude, and connection with our Creator. We believe worship is a powerful way to express our love, gratitude, and reverence for God. It prepares our hearts for the Word, strengthens our faith, and draws us closer to the Lord and to one another. Together, we worship in unity—young and old, new believers and seasoned saints—because worship is for everyone.
