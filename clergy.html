Ven. Canon Alfred Muriuki

Ve. Canon Alfred Muriuki has served as the Vicar at ACK St. Faith’s Church since May 2025. He is passionate about discipleship and community outreach.

Rev. Lucy Wambaa

Rev. Lucy Wambaa serves faithfully at ACK St. Faith’s Church and has been instrumental in pastoral care and church programs. Her ministry focuses on community outreach and mentorship. She also serves in Children's ministry.

Canon Peter Mbugua

Canon Peter Mbugua brings years of wisdom and leadership to ACK St. Faith’s Church. He plays a key role in church governance and spiritual formation.

Capt. Johnstone Mukoya

Capt. Johnstone Mukoya is a dedicated layreader at ACK St. Faith’s Church. He is involved in various church activities and community service.

Capt. Jean Karari

Capt. Jean Karari is a committed layreader at ACK St. Faith’s Church. She actively participates in church services and community outreach programs.

Leader One

Leader One serves as a dedicated church elder, supporting the church’s strategic direction and helping to guide the congregation in spiritual matters.
