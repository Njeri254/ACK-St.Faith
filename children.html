Children Ministry

About the Children Ministry

The church school is a ministry whose goal is to teach liturgy and provide guidance to children aged between 2 and 12 years. Ms. Pauline Juma is the coordinator. The ministry offers holistic education during Sunday services and organizes various activities throughout the year to promote spiritual growth and fellowship.

Class Divisions

  • Ages 2–4 years (Madonye)
  • Ages 5–6 years
  • Class/Grade 1–3
  • Class/Grade 4–5
  • Pre-teens class

Volunteers & Teachers

The ministry has over 30 volunteers who assist with teaching and class management. Volunteers must be saved and passionate about helping children grow in Christ. You may serve for a year, two years, or as a lifelong volunteer. Join us!

Teachers' Training

We believe in continuous sharpening of our skills. ACK St. Faith’s Church organizes teacher training sessions every year.

Classes/Services

Church school classes are held every Sunday at 9:00 AM and run concurrently with the main service.

Activities

  • Vacation Bible Study (VBS) – week-long Bible learning during holidays
  • Talent shows and memory verse competitions
  • Annual fun day and retreats
  • Birthday celebrations and outreach events

ACK St. Faith's Church is committed to spreading the Gospel, building strong Christian families, and serving the community with love and truth.
