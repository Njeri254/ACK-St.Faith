Mission Team

Sharing Christ's Love Through Action

Our Mission

As a dedicated team, the Mission Team is driven by the need to follow in Christ's footsteps as instructed in Luke 6:27-30. Our goal is to reach the lost, the needy, and the vulnerable in society and share the Gospel of Christ to all corners of the world ( Matthew 28:19-20). We believe in promoting unity and the well-being of the entire community.

Director of Missions

Eng. George Dawo

Director of Missions

Eng. George Dawo

Eng. Dawo has faithfully led the Mission Team for over 8 years, with a heart for outreach, discipleship, and spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the vulnerable. He coordinates mission activities, oversees local and regional outreach, and ensures that the love of Christ is shared practically and spiritually.
